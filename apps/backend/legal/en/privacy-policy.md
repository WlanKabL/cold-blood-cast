## 3. What Data We Collect

- **Account data:** Username, email address, and encrypted password when you register

- **Trading data:** Trade entries, journal notes, account configurations, and prop firm details you enter into the Service

- **Log data:** IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, date and time of access

### 3.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect payment card details directly (processed by third-party payment providers)
- We do **not** have access to your brokerage accounts for trading purposes and do **not** execute trades on your behalf
- We do **not** collect or store your broker master password — only read-only investor passwords, encrypted at rest

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on servers located within the **European Union**
- Passwords are hashed using **PBKDF2** with an additional pepper — we cannot read your password
- Broker investor passwords are encrypted with **AES-256** at rest
- All data transmission is encrypted via **TLS/HTTPS**
- Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel only
- We conduct regular security reviews and keep dependencies up to date

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Account data** is retained for as long as your account is active
- **Trading data** is retained for as long as your account is active, and deleted upon account deletion
- **Log data** is retained for a maximum of **90 days**
- **Backup data** may persist for up to **30 days** after deletion before being purged
- After account deletion, we may retain certain data as required by applicable tax and commercial law (up to 10 years for financial records under German HGB/AO)
