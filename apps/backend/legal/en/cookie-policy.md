## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to the website operators.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

Cold Blood Cast uses cookies for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the operation of the Service. They enable core functionality such as:

| Cookie          | Purpose                               | Duration   |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------- | ---------- |
| `access_token`  | JWT access token for authentication   | 15 minutes |
| `refresh_token` | JWT refresh token for session renewal | 7 days     |

These cookies are **HTTP-only** and **secure**, meaning they cannot be accessed by JavaScript and are only transmitted over encrypted connections. Without these cookies, the Service cannot function, and they cannot be disabled.

### 2.2 Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your settings and preferences:

| Cookie   | Purpose                                   | Duration |
| -------- | ----------------------------------------- | -------- |
| `locale` | Stores your language preference (en/de)   | 1 year   |
| `theme`  | Stores your theme preference (dark/light) | 1 year   |

### 2.3 Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with the Service. These cookies collect information anonymously and help us improve the user experience.

We currently do **not** use third-party analytics services. If this changes, this policy will be updated accordingly.

### 2.4 Advertising Cookies

Cold Blood Cast does **not** use advertising or tracking cookies. We do not serve ads and do not share your data with advertising networks.

## 3. Third-Party Cookies

The Service does not embed third-party content that sets cookies. If this changes in the future (e.g., embedded payment provider widgets), this policy will be updated.

## 4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

- View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
- Block third-party cookies
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Block all cookies
- Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that blocking strictly necessary cookies will prevent the Service from functioning properly.

### Browser-Specific Instructions

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- **Edge:** Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions
