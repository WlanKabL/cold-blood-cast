This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") sets out the rules for using the Cold Blood Cast platform at cold-blood-cast.app (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to comply with this policy.

This AUP is part of our **Terms of Service**. Violations of this policy may result in suspension or termination of your account.

## 2. Permitted Use

The Service is intended for:

- Logging and documenting your personal trading activity
- Analyzing your trading performance with the provided analytics tools
- Managing prop firm accounts and tracking challenge progress
- Storing trading-related notes and journal entries

## 3. Prohibited Activities

You agree **not** to use the Service to:

- Attempt to bypass authentication or security mechanisms

- Access accounts, data, or systems that do not belong to you
- Introduce malware, viruses, worms, trojans, or other malicious code

- Create multiple accounts to circumvent usage limits or bans

- Impersonate any person, entity, or organization
- Misrepresent your identity or affiliation
- Provide false or misleading information during registration or use

- Copy, modify, or distribute the Service's code, design, or content without authorization
